About Abebayo Akinfenwa

Abebayo Akinfenwa was born in Wimbledon, England. He participated in the 2004 Olympic games and came 14th, and he later went to Beijing in 2008 and came 13th. He retired from professional weightlifting in 2010 and has been teaching a weightlifting class in highschool.
